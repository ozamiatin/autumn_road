Autumn Road Game

This is a road puzzle game which includes:

  • Road building using 20+ road blocks
  • Control over crossroads manual and auto
  • Infinite number of randomized levels each could become quite a complicated problem to solve

In order to win the round need to join at least 50% of road entries and have positive money balance before round time is up.
